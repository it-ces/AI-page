Moving avarage
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Take in mind the simple mean (unweighted).

+---------+------------+------------+
|    X    | X.shift(1) | X.shift(2) |
+---------+------------+------------+
| $x_{1}$ |     NaN    |     NaN    |
+---------+------------+------------+
| $x_{2}$ |   $x_{1}$  |     NaN    |
+---------+------------+------------+
| $x_{3}$ |   $x_{2}$  |   $x_{1}$  |
+---------+------------+------------+
| $x_{4}$ |   $x_{3}$  |   $x_{2}$  |
+---------+------------+------------+
| $x_{5}$ |   $x_{4}$  |   $x_{3}$  |
+---------+------------+------------+



Then you can construct a sum of each column.

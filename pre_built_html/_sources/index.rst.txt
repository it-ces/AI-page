Data Analysis project
=============================
Hi, I’m an economist with a master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence, with a strong background in statistics, optimization, and machine learning.

I am currently improving educational materials on my platform, *ai-page.readthedocs.io*, where I publish content on probability, statistics, machine learning, microeconomics, and metaheuristics, among other topics.


In this web-site include material, for some courses.

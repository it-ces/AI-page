Iván Trujillo Abella
====================

Economist, M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence, and currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Mathematics at the Pontificia Universidad Javeriana. His interests include optimization, machine learning, agent-based modeling, game theory, and quantitative methods for decision-making.

This microsite presents educational materials, research projects, and teaching resources in statistics, economics, data science, and artificial intelligence, with practical implementations in Python and evolutionary computation.

Website: ai-page.readthedocs.io

About this site
---------------

This platform provides educational content on:

- Probability and statistics  
- Machine learning  
- Optimization and metaheuristics  
- Game theory and Agent-Based models.

Areas of specialization
-----------------------

- Machine Learning applied to Economics  
- Metaheuristic Optimization (Genetic Algorithms, Grey Wolf Optimizer, Ant Colony, ...)  
- Agent-Based Modeling (ABM)  
- Credit Risk Modeling  
- Game Theory  


Topics
=========================

* Data mining: 
   * Clustering
   * Apriori

* Machine learning:
   * Linear Regression
   * Logistic Regression
   * Neural Networks
   * Support Vector Machine

* Genetic algorithms

* Probability and statistics:
   * Hypothesis testing
   * Comparing groups  

* Economics:
   * Microeconomics
   * Economic Growth

Iván Trujillo Abella
====================

Economist and M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence, with a strong background in statistics, optimization, machine learning, and credit risk.

This microsite presents the academic work and teaching methodology of Iván Trujillo Abella, including materials in Python, Stata, and evolutionary algorithms applied to economics, data science, and agent-based modeling.

Website: https://ai-page.readthedocs.io/


About this site
---------------

This platform provides educational content on:

- Probability and statistics  
- Machine learning  
- Optimization and metaheuristics  
- Game theory and Agent-Based models.

Areas of specialization
-----------------------

- Machine Learning applied to Economics  
- Metaheuristic Optimization (Genetic Algorithms, Grey Wolf Optimizer, Ant Colony, ...)  
- Agent-Based Modeling (ABM)  
- Credit Risk Modeling  
- Game Theory  


Topics
=========================

* Data mining: 
   * Clustering
   * Apriori

* Machine learning:
   * Linear Regression
   * Logistic Regression
   * Neural Networks
   * Support Vector Machine

* Genetic algorithms

* Probability and statistics:
   * Hypothesis testing
   * Comparing groups  

* Economics:
   * Microeconomics
   * Economic Growth
